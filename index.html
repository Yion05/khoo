Our Story

Welcome to PD Leisure Homestay, your ultimate home away from home nestled in the beautiful coastal town of Port Dickson. We are passionately dedicated to creating a serene and comfortable retreat perfect for families, couples and solo adventurers alike who are seeking a truly memorable beachfront experience. Our accommodations are thoughtfully designed with your relaxation in mind, offering the perfect fusion of modern amenities and a cozy, inviting atmosphere, ensuring your stay is nothing short of exceptional.

Conveniently located in Port Dickson, our homestay offers easy access to several key attractions, situated 30 km from Palm Mall Seremban and 36 km from the Sepang International Circuit. Each of our holiday homes features three bedrooms, two bathrooms, fresh bedding, towels, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a dining area, and a fully equipped kitchen. Guests can also enjoy complimentary WiFi and the convenience of private on-site parking.

Whether you are looking for a peaceful escape or an adventure-filled vacation, PD Leisure Homestay is the ideal choice. On-site, guests can enjoy a friendly game of darts, or venture out to explore the scenic surroundings by cycling. We eagerly look forward to welcoming you and making your visit to Port Dickson a truly unforgettable experience.

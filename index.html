Our Story

Welcome to PD Leisure Homestay, your home away from home in the beautiful coastal town of Port Dickson. We are dedicated to providing a serene and comfortable retreat for families, couples, and solo travelers seeking a memorable beachfront experience. Our homestays are designed with your relaxation in mind, offering a perfect blend of modern amenities and a cozy, inviting atmosphere.

Founded with a passion for hospitality and a deep love for Port Dickson's natural beauty, PD Leisure Homestay aims to create lasting memories for our guests. We believe in offering personalized service and ensuring every detail of your stay is perfect, from the moment you arrive until your departure.

Whether you're looking for a peaceful escape or an adventure-filled vacation, PD Leisure Homestay is the ideal choice. We look forward to welcoming you and making your stay in Port Dickson truly unforgettable.
